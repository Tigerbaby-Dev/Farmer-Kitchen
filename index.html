Our Mission

Grown in Oregon.
Delivered with honesty.

Farmer's Kitchen was built on a single belief: the people who grow our food deserve to be properly supported, and the people who eat it deserve to know exactly where it comes from.

We partner exclusively with small, independent farms across the Willamette Valley — paying above-market rates, eliminating unnecessary markup, and building lasting relationships rooted in transparency.

Why Choose Us

The way food
should work.

We've rethought every step of how food moves from field to table. Each decision — from how we source to how we pay — reflects a commitment to doing things right.

01

Direct from local farmers

No distributors. No brokers. Food travels from a Willamette Valley field directly to your kitchen, preserving freshness and traceability.

02

Organic & sustainable

Every partner farm follows certified organic or regenerative practices — better for your family and for Oregon's land and waterways.

03

Fair support for farmers

We pay above-market rates, always. Farmers reinvest in their land and families — and in the next generation of Oregon agriculture.

04

Radical transparency

Every product tells you which farm it came from, who grew it, and how it was raised. Real food starts with honest information.
